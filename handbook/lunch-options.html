Lunch options

Current lunch options within walking distance include:

  • Oriste Cafe (deep discount for students)
  • Parakeet Cafe
  • Cargo Cafe

More info can be found on the Scripps Intranet Cafeterias page, including about the fresh food vending machines. In addition, the lab break room has a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee machine.
